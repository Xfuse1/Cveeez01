# **App Name**: CVeeez Crafted Careers

## Core Features:

- AI CV Builder: Utilize AI to generate professional CVs based on user inputs, including skills, experience, and desired job roles.
- E-commerce Platform: Enable users to purchase services, such as premium CV templates, career counseling, and other resources. This involves secure payment processing and order management.
- User Dashboard: Provide a personalized dashboard where users can track their CV applications, manage their profile, and access purchased services.
- Talent Space: Create a platform where job seekers can showcase their talents and connect with potential employers.
- Job Board Portal: Aggregate job postings from various sources and allow users to search and apply for jobs directly through the platform.
- Admin Dashboard: A protected admin area to manage the content of the site, user accounts and permissions.
- GDPR Compliance Tool: As a tool, this feature helps ensure GDPR compliance, including consent management, data access requests, and privacy policy adherence.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#00BFFF) to evoke trust, professionalism, and clarity.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E6F7FF), creating a clean and calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant blue (#1E90FF) to highlight important CTAs and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif providing a balance of modernity and warmth for readability.
- Use professional and consistent icons that align with career and talent themes, ensuring they are clear and intuitive.
- Design a clear and intuitive layout that guides users through the platform effectively. Prioritize key information and make navigation straightforward.
- Incorporate subtle and professional animations to enhance user experience, such as loading animations or smooth transitions between pages.
